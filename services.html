What we do

Year-round exterior services for Minnesota properties

The Groundsmasters keeps your property looking sharp across seasons with irrigation, mowing, concrete curbing, flatwork, and more — all tuned for Southern Minnesota’s weather and soil.

Core services we specialize in

We focus on work that makes the biggest impact on curb appeal, safety, and maintenance time — especially for homeowners, HOAs, and commercial properties in Owatonna, Rochester, and nearby communities.

  • Designed for Minnesota freeze–thaw cycles
  • Efficient routing and scheduling for recurring work
  • Clear estimates and communication before we start

Irrigation Services

Keep turf and beds healthy without wasting water. We repair, optimize, and protect irrigation systems so they perform reliably all season.

  • Spring irrigation startups
  • Head adjustments & coverage optimization
  • Leak & break repairs
  • Controller programming & smart controller upgrades
  • Fall blowouts / winterization

Best for: HOAs, commercial turf, and homeowners who want consistent, even coverage.

Mowing & Grounds Care

Clean, consistent mowing and seasonal cleanups that keep entrances, common areas, and front yards looking maintained.

  • Weekly mowing & trimming
  • Bed edging and touch-ups
  • Spring & fall cleanups
  • Basic turf care recommendations

Best for: Residential properties, small businesses, and HOA common areas.

Concrete Landscape Curbing

Permanent concrete edging that sharpens bed lines, reduces mulch washout, and makes mowing faster and cleaner.

  • Decorative & smooth curbing profiles
  • Color and texture options
  • Driveway & walk transition edging
  • Bed reshaping and prep

Best for: Homeowners and HOAs wanting a finished look with less trimming.

Concrete Flatwork & Pads

Durable flatwork for high-traffic and utility areas — poured and finished with drainage and long-term performance in mind.

  • Equipment pads & utility slabs
  • Walkways and small patios
  • Apron replacements & transitions
  • Selective flatwork repairs

Best for: Shops, light commercial sites, and residential upgrades.

Additional seasonal & support services

Drainage & grading

Light grading and basic drainage solutions tied into your landscape work.

Snow & ice (select clients)

Plowing, sidewalk clearing, and de-icing for qualifying commercial and HOA accounts.

Lighting (partnered)

Path and accent lighting, often paired with curbing and concrete projects.
